Watch it Ouside Boston Edition

Watch it Ouside Boston Edition is an outdoor film festival in Boston. Watch it outside events Co's mission is to provide a free screening of films to the public in Boston in order to promote public interest in the films. Also to draw the world's attention to Boston as a tourist center of cinematography. He wants to create an event that shows movies can appeal to audiences of all ages, and wants his commercials. Watch it outside events Co, is a non-profit organization.
